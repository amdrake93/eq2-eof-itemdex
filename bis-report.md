## Assumptions & Constants

- crit ×1.50; flurry ×4.0; ability-mod adds in full (50% cap disproven by tooltip probes)
- haste & dps-mod: fitted quadratic 0.800348·s − 0.00127275·s², hard cap 300 stat → 125%
- main stat (AGI): interpolated live-reading curve — 65% at 1100, flat plateau ~1100–1200, then a second regime to 80% at 1661 (clamps above); multiplies BOTH CA and auto-attack damage (same curve)
- CA potency pool includes a calibrated `potency_bonus` (~24.6) — a Wuoshi TLE server damage adjustment (survives naked/AA-less/buff-less; captured empirically, spec §12)
- reuse: DIVIDES recast by (1 + reuse/100), floored at each art's 50%-of-base recast (reached at 100% reuse; AA art mods sit at that floor — Assassinate/Mortal Blade); cast speed divides cast times; recovery base 0.50s (reduced by recovery speed); fight target = 600s (smoothed)
- Set built by coordinate-ascent to convergence (caps/interactions resolved at the live set baseline).
- Main-hand is fixed (Soulfire Sabre); its weapon damage AND full stat line are included in the baseline.
